Public Repository


Achintya
Science communicator at CERN and PhD student in scicomm at UWE Bristol. gave his community a central place to organize around. Learned to use GitHub for project management
We know that cosmic rays are high-energy particles that bombard the earth’s atmosphere and produce showers of particles that we can detect on the surface of the planet. CRAYFIS - turn your phone into a cosmic ray detector. Physicists can learn a lot about our Universe and the laws governing it by studying these particle showers. Open Cosmics is meant to foster conversation and collaboration. We want to bring together the many cosmic-ray projects to discuss their scope and needs. Open Cosmics is evaluating several data formats, such as FITS and .ig. CERN IT, CosmicPi, CERN at school, CRAYFIS
